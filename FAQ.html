FAQ

When payment is not required, why do I need to file a report?

If we are mailng you a report form, your status is active for that agreement. Not filing a report for an active agreement will jeopardize your right to request and receive a Trust Fund Clearance. The missed report will mean your account is no longer current. Even though a report for 0 hours can be delinquent there will never be a delinquency invoice for it.

Should we have no foreseeable union work in Hawaii, can we be inactive?

There is a check box on most of the monthly report forms for this, but we prefer that you send us the request in writing. If you have more than one active agreement you can be selective about the agreement you would like to be inacitve. If you have just one active agreement, inactive status would mean closing your account with us and doing a closing audit. While you are still a signatory to HRCC you can re-open your account with a simple written request.

What will be billed on a delinquent report?

You will be billed for the Liquidated Damages which is $20.00 or 20% of the contribution, whichever is greater, Fund Interest at a rate of 12% per annum and Lost Earnings on 401k benefit as calculated on the VFCP Calculator website. Any short payment will be included in the bill as a discrepancy and will be included in the basis of all penalty charges.
